# HackAtlas

### Mapping the world's top hackathon builders.

---

# What is HackAtlas?

- A platform to map top hackathon talent and builders.
- Helps founders and teams discover strong collaborators early.
- Improves your odds of building a winning hackathon team.

---

# Why build this?

- **Find winners**: winners beget more winners.
- **Interview winners**: study their winning playbooks and repeatable strategies.
- **Build leverage**:
  - Build long-term relationships with proven builders.
  - Form faster, higher-quality teams for future hackathons.
  - Turn one event into a compounding network and execution advantage.

---

# Who is this for?

- People who want to use data to prepare early before hackathons.
- People who want to win hackathons.
- People who want to build leverage through connections and projects after hackathons.

---

# Closing

HackAtlas turns scattered hackathon outcomes into a talent map you can act on.

### Find the right people. Build smarter teams. Win more together.
